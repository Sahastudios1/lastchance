Mission and Vision Statement

At Tamale Islamic Science Senior High School, we strive to provide a nurturing and inclusive learning environment, empowering students with knowledge, skills, and values to become responsible global citizens committed to serving humanity

History and Background

Tamale Islamic Science Senior High School, established in 1992, has a rich history as a prominent educational institution in Ghana. Founded with the goal of blending Islamic principles with modern education, the school has since become a beacon of academic excellence, moral values, and community service. Over the years, it has nurtured generations of students, empowering them to make meaningful contributions to society while upholding the motto of "Supplication to God and Service to Humanity.

Profile of the School
